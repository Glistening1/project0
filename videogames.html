Favorite Video Games

I play a lot of video games. Their something I've always been passionate about in playing. Here is a collection of the ones I personally enjoyed the most.


Persona 5

The latest entry to the main line Persona series and a critically acclaimed RPG in 2016, Persona 5 takes the you into the life as a Phantom Thief. Here in this game you take on Palace rulers as you take their hearts to reform and bring them to justice.

Ni No Kuni II: Revenant Kindom

The very hyped Sequel to the first game of the series Ni No Kuni: Wrath of the White Witch. The main protaganist this time around is the young King Evan. The former ruler of Ding Dong Dell, Evan was taken from power due to a coup on his country. Now he has a set up a new kindom located in the middle of Heartlands and partnered up with Roland and friends in an effort to bring peace to the world of Ni No Kuni.

Pokemon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers Of Sky

An enchanced version of the second entry to the Pokemon Mystery Dungeon series and personally one of my fondest video games experiences, Explorers of Sky takes you on a journey in a parallel world entirely habitated with creatues known as Pokemon. The main character was a human turned into a Pokemon that washed up on a beach near a local town. There he/she has a chance encounter with another Pokemon. They both become friends and join the local guild known as Wigglytuff's Guild.

Xenoblade Chronicles 2

The sequel to the RPG series Xenoblade Chronicles, this new version sees the player taking control of Rex, a salvager at the Argentum Trade Guild. In the world, Alrest, the major contintents are on large creatures known as Titans. Though, the Titans are slowly dying and Rex hopes that they can find an area known as Elysium so that everyone could live without the fear of the Titans dying out. A chance encounter with the legendary Aegis, named Pyra, the two set out together to find Elysium.

Ghost Trick: Phantom Detective

Published by Capcom, Ghost Trick Phantom Detective is a hidden gem in the rough features on the Nintendo DS. The main protaginist, named Sissel, finds himself in a junkyard outside of town. Though it is at this very junkyard Sissel finds himself dead on the pavement. He is able to use his powers he got after death, or "Ghost Tricks," to save a red-haired girl named Lynne. Now he sets off to find out the reason he was killed before sunrise or else he will disappear before he can find the truth.

FTL: Faster Than Light

Faster Than Light, better known as FTL, is a game published by Subset Games. In this, you hold the vital piece of information to save the Federation from the pursuing Rebel Fleet. The player then commands their very own spaceship and sets off into the galaxy to make it to home base before the Rebels completely destroy it.
